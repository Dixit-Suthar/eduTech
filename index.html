Subtraction

Multiplication

In mathematics, multiplication is a method of finding the product of two or more numbers. It is one of the basic arithmetic operations, that we use in everyday life.

Division

In maths, a division is a process of splitting a specific amount into equal parts. For example, we can divide a group of 20 members into 4 groups of 5 members each or 5 groups of 4 members each, and so on.
